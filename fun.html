Abenezer Ayana's Interests

Cooking

During my free time, I enjoy cooking multi-cultural cuisines. I watch cooking videos from BuzzFeed's Tasty app and YouTube, and prepare meals that are cost-friendly. My favorite and most recent dessert I prepared was a Chocholate Bowl. I used a pudding holder to place a melted dark chocholate and froze it for 3 hours. I then carefully extracted the molded chocholate and poured melted white chocholate on the bottom. This created a unique design which I solidified by freezing it for an additional hour. Finally, I filled the bowl with chocholate pudding, chocholate chips and strawberries! It was absolutely delicious!!! Below are some of the dishes I've created in the past.
